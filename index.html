Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-15

This Privacy Policy describes how TechKE Daily ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the TechKE Daily mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: name, email address, and profile details if you sign in.
  • Usage data: app interactions, feature usage, and performance metrics.
  • Device data: device identifiers, OS version, language, and app version.
  • Content data: bookmarks, likes, and preferences you save in the App.
  • Purchase data: records of in-app purchases and restore events.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App experience.
  • Sync your saved items across devices.
  • Improve features, performance, and reliability.
  • Process in-app purchases and restore purchases.
  • Show ads and measure ad performance when ads are enabled.

Ads and Analytics

We use third-party services to provide ads and measurement, such as Google AdMob. These providers may collect device identifiers and usage data to deliver and measure ads. You can remove ads via the in-app purchase, if available.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to host data and provide authentication and analytics. These services may process your information on our behalf. Examples include:

  • Supabase (authentication and data storage)
  • Google AdMob (ads)
  • App store billing providers (in-app purchases)

Data Retention

We keep information as long as needed to provide the App or as required by law. You can request deletion of your account data by contacting us.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

  • You can update or remove saved content within the App.
  • You can request account deletion by contacting us.
  • You can disable ads by purchasing the ad-free option, if available.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date when changes are made.

Contact Us

If you have questions or requests, contact us at:

Email: qlanlabs@gmail.com
